Backstory

In the spring of 2017, Joseph Sorensen was assigned a project in his computer Science class. He was to create a game in HTML and JavaScript using p5js. He rose to the occasion and created Galactic Armada, an extremely simple game.

A few months later, Joe found the game again and thought to himself, "I could do better". So, he began work on a new Galactic Armada, and here he is now.
