About galactic armada

The original Galactic Armada game was originally developed by Joseph Sorensen as a Computer Science project in early 2017.

Since Joe was only allowed to use one library for his game, he believed he could rewrite the entire game, and make it 1,000,000 times better. Hence, this site was created to be the home of his creation.
